About Me

Hi! My name is Marie Joyce Tio, but I prefer to be called Joyce. I am 19 years old. I recently graduated from Glendale High School in 2016. In high school, I was a 4-year member of Glendale High School Dance/Drill Team, and won multiple national titles. What got me interested in coding? My sister is a computer engineer, and I've always been interested in computers since I was younger.

In my spare time, I like to sleep in to catch up on rest. I also like to take dance classes with my friends, read, edit music and videos, or code. I enjoy hanging out with friends and trying out new kinds of food. My favorite color is blue, but I also like the color black. I like the color blue so much that my friends called my birthday "Blue Day". Finally, I also think that writing about myself is harder than coding.
